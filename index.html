IEEE Regional Exemplary Student Branch Award 2022

This certificate recognizes SIES Graduate School of Technology for receiving the IEEE Regional Exemplary Student Branch Award 2022. It highlights the branch’s outstanding performance in organizing technical programs, activities, and networking opportunities that help members develop critical skills. The award was presented by IEEE President K. J. Ray Liu on 8th October 2022.

Best Student Chapter/Society award under the IEEE Bombay Section’s

This certificate recognizes MTTS - SIES Graduate School of Technology for winning the Best Student Chapter/Society award under the IEEE Bombay Section’s Volunteers, Committee, and Affinity Group Awards 2021. It acknowledges their commendable performance and volunteer efforts in uplifting and bettering the section, contributing to its growth. The certificate is signed by Dr. Anjali Deshpande (Chair, Award Committee) and Dr. Satyanarayana Bheesette (Chair, IEEE Bombay Section).

IEEE Regional Exemplary Student Branch Award 2021

This certificate honors SIES Graduate School of Technology for receiving the IEEE Regional Exemplary Student Branch Award 2021 under the Bombay Section, Region 10 (R10). It acknowledges the branch's outstanding performance in hosting technical programs, activities, and networking opportunities that help members develop critical skills. The award was presented on 20th December 2021 and signed by Susan K. (Kathy) Land, IEEE President 2021.
