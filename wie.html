WOMEN IN EMGINEERING

"Meet Our IEEE Behind The Scene Members"

The Women in Engineering (WiE) affinity group at IEEE SIESGST is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in the field of engineering and technology. This track aims to empower women engineers by providing a supportive community where they can connect, collaborate, and excel. WiE focuses on topics such as ethical AI, neuro AI, digital artwork, and leadership, offering a platform for personal and professional growth. At IEEE SIESGST, the WiE track has been instrumental in organizing impactful events like the International Women’s Day seminar and panel discussions. These initiatives inspire students by showcasing the achievements of women in technology and providing resources to overcome challenges in the field. WiE fosters an environment of equality, encouraging all students to thrive and make meaningful contributions.
