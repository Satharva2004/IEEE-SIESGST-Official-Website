MICROWAVE THEORY AND TECHNOLOGY SOCIETY

"Meet Our IEEE Behind The Scene Members"

The Microwave Theory and Techniques Society (MTTS) at IEEE SIESGST focuses on the research and development of advanced communication technologies. This track covers a wide range of topics, including microwave engineering, antennas, deep space communication, radar systems, and wireless technology.

MTTS provides students with the tools and knowledge required to delve into the intricacies of modern communication systems, fostering innovation in the field.

At IEEE SIESGST, the MTTS track stands out for its hands-on workshops, technical seminars, and collaborative projects. Events such as Epsilon provide students with exposure to state-of-the-art advancements in space communication and signal processing, encouraging them to tackle complex challenges and contribute to the future of communication technologies.
