COMPUTER SOCIETY

"Meet Our IEEE Behind The Scene Members"

The Computer Society (CSI Chapter) of IEEE SIESGST is dedicated to advancing knowledge in the field of computing and IT. This track provides a platform for students to explore cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, software development, and computer vision. Through workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, and expert talks, the CSI Chapter empowers students to develop their technical skills and stay updated with the latest industry trends. At IEEE SIESGST, the CSI Chapter has consistently driven innovation and learning, offering opportunities for members to collaborate, research, and implement real-world solutions. By connecting students with industry leaders and fostering peer-to-peer learning, this track plays a pivotal role in preparing the next generation of technology professionals.
